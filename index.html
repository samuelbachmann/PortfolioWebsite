About me

I am Samuel Bachmann. I am 18 years old and am currently in the coderclass. The coderclass is a special program from the Metis Montessori lyceum in Amsterdam. In this program you learn different programming languages and about embedded systems. During the last three years you can choose to do your own projects. You can see the projects down below.

Outside of school I enjoy going back to my family in the Austrian alps and hiking the mountains and skiing the slopes. I hope to study in Austria. I also find it interesting to learn practical skills like welding and smithing.

In the past I have had many different jobs I worked at the Albert Heijn and Deliveroo in the Netherlands, I have also had different summer jobs working at a ski lift in Austria and the production line of electrical chargers at Enercharge.

Fuzzing challenge VU

My high school the Metis Montessori lyceum Amsterdam in cooperation with VUSEC a cyber security department with in the Vrije Universiteit Amsterdam offers a course about fuzzing. Fuzzing in short is providing invalid or unexpected inputs to see how the program handles it and if it crashes.

After successfully completing the Fuzzing course, I received an official certificate from VUSEC.


C++ course with the VU
