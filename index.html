About me

I am Samuel Bachmann. I am 17 years old and am currently in the codderclass. The coderclass is a special program from the Metis Montessori lyceum in Amsterdam. In this program you learn different programing languages and about embeded systems. During the last three years you can choose to do youre own projects. You can see the projects down below.

Outside of school i enjoy going back to my family in the Austrian alps and hiking the mountains and skiing the slopes. I hope to study in Austria. I also find it really interessting to learn pratical skills like welding and smithing.

In the past i have had many different jobs i worked at the Albert Heijn and Deliveroo in the netherlands, i have also had different summer jobs working at a ski lift in austria and the production line of electrical chargers at Enercharge.
